The delivery model of PaaS is similar to SaaS, except instead of delivering the software over the internet, PaaS provides a platform for software creation. This platform is delivered over the web, and gives developers the freedom to concentrate on building the software while still not having to worry about operating systems, software updates, storage, or infrastructure.PaaS allows businesses to design and create applications that are built into the PaaS with special software components. These applications,or middleware, are scalable and highly available as they take on certain cloud characteristics.
